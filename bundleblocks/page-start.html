Solutions

Business Solutions

To facilitate the business with tooling with very specific requirements, constraints or functionalities, a formal system is either too expensive, to rigid or not available. In these cases low code and no code solutions in Microsoft 365 offers an alternative.

Collaboration, DMS and ECM

This area includes services related to enabling technologies like the ones offered in Microsoft 365. This targets the leveraging of the platform generic functionalities instead of combining functionalities to a particular solution.

IT Services

Infrastructure, Platform and Solutions as a service are purchased instead of provided. Competences in selecting, integrating, governing and adoption of these tools and equipment become core activities, while other classic internal IT responsibilities decrease in greater or lesser extent. DTS aims to deliver the knowledge and materials to apply Microsoft cloud services.
